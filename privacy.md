# Privacy Policy

Shadow Browser Sensor collects metadata-only browser telemetry for security and compliance monitoring.

## What We Collect
- Domain names visited
- Application identifiers
- Event timestamps
- Device and tenant identifiers

## What We Do NOT Collect
- Page content
- Keystrokes
- Credentials
- Form inputs
- Personal communications

## Data Usage
Collected data is sent securely to a customer-controlled backend for security analysis and compliance purposes.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share collected data with third parties.

## Data Retention
Data retention is controlled by the customer environment.
